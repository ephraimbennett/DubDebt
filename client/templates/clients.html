Our Solutions

Three Click Settlements
DubDebt provides a smooth, secure infrastructure in order to get your clients to settle debts in just three clicks. No complicated registrations, no paperwork, no hassle. Studies show a trustworthy platform can increase repayment rates by over 300%.
Personalized Reachout
Using advanced AI analytics, we craft strategic messaging to individuals. As a licensed debt recovery assistor, we contact your debtors with respect and trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

DubDebt is a modern SaaS platform that empowers businesses to collect unpaid fees and manage accounts receivable digitally. We streamline debt recovery and payment for you and your clients.
You securely upload your outstanding receivables. DubDebt automatically notifies your clients, offering them a simple way to pay or start a payment plan online. You track all activity from your dashboard.
Security is at our core: DubDebt is SOC2 certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never resell or share your business or consumer data—period.
Our Basic plan is pay-as-you-go, charging 20% of revenue recovered. Our Pro service plan is 5% of revenue recovered plus a subscription fee. See our pricing section for details.
With DubDebt, you retain full control. There’s no aggressive third-party collector—your brand, your client relationship. Plus, our platform is digital, transparent, and designed for modern business.
Funds are either deposited directly to your designated business account, less our fee via our partnership with Stripe Connect. You see every transaction in your dashboard, in real time. Alternatively, payments made to DubDebt could be electronically deposited into our trust accounts. We would then provides client remittances on a monthly basis via electronic ACH transfer or, Paypal, or if necessary, by paper check.
