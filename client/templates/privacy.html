Privacy & Data Protection at DubDebt

At DubDebt, your privacy is our top priority. We build our platform on transparency, control, and the highest standards of security.
SOC2 Certified GDPR Compliant

You own your data

We never resell or share your business or client information. Your data stays yours—period.

What Information We Collect

To help your business collect unpaid fees efficiently and securely, DubDebt collects:

  • Business account info: Name, business contact, address, payment information, and login credentials.
  • Debtor/customer data: Information you provide about your clients to process collections (names, contact details, debt amounts, repayment status).
  • Platform usage data: Actions taken within the platform for product improvement and support (never sold, never used for advertising).

How We Use Your Data

  • To power debt collection workflows for your business.
  • To provide you and your team with secure account access and customer support.
  • To notify debtors and manage communications (on your behalf—never for DubDebt’s own marketing).
  • To improve our services, always in an aggregated, non-identifiable way.

How We Keep Data Secure

  • All data encrypted at rest and in transit, using modern industry standards.
  • Access controls ensure only your team can access your business and debtor data.
  • Regular third-party audits and certifications, including SOC2 and GDPR compliance.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, export, or delete your account and debtor data at any time.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate business or customer information.
  • Contact us for privacy questions or data requests: team@dubdebt.com
